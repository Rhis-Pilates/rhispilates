Hi I'm Rhi!

Growing up in a holistic household, pilates and healthy living have been a part of my life for as long as I can remember. Transitioning from competitive dancing to pilates, I aspire to bring a fun & upbeat experience to my students, grounded in detail-oriented classical approach. Upon receiving my pilates certification through Core Pilates NYC, I have been blessed to spend the past 5 years teaching pilates across Manhattan at Equinox & Uptown Pilates locations.

Whether you are new to pilates or an advanced student, I will work with you to tailor a custom program that addresses your specific goals. I have experience teaching my students through pilates to improve their core strength, flexibility, mental health, overcome injuries, and prepare for child-birth. There is nothing more important than the mind-body connection and with a little bit of effort, I can challenge you to become the best version of yourself, while having fun doing it!

If you want to see if I’m the right instructor for you or just learn a little more about pilates in general, Ask Rhi!
